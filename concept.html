Concept

The Female Gaze Archive examines how image construction produces authority. It studies framing, posture, styling, silence and restraint as deliberate instruments of control.

Rather than focusing on narrative plot, this archive isolates visual moments. Each entry anaylzyes how composition and aesthetic discipline shape perception.

The project treats image as structure - not decoration - and positions femininity as strategic rather than passive.
