Method

Entries are selected based on precision rather than narrative popularity. The archive prioritizes scenes where posture, framing, costume, and spatial positioning are deliberately controlled.

Each case study isolates a single image and examines how compositional choices - distance, symmetry, gesture and stillness construct authority.

The method focuses on visual discipline: how restraint within the frame guides interpretation.
